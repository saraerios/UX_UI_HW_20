Home Page

NueTravel allows users to navigate to a tutorial or go straight to login.

Problem Statement

We believe that creating a travel planning app with achieve a seamless travel opportunity for users. Current travel sites were designed to achieve only one goal per site. We have observed that current travel sites aren’t meeting the goal of comprehensive travel planning, leaving travelers to spend unnecessary time and mental energy trying to organize their trips in an unpredictable world. This causes undue burden on the traveler, and makes a vacation feel like work. How might we improve travel planning options for young adults and create a seamless journey for them, based upon return users?

Iterations

To make information more clear, the CTA was made larger and more clear to help users understand what buttons to press and when.
